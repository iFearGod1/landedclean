Transaction Process & Risk Mitigation

All domain transfers are conducted exclusively through a licensed third-party escrow service to ensure transactional integrity.

Use of escrow protects both parties by:

  • Verifying domain ownership
  • Holding funds in trust pending transfer
  • Eliminating payment reversals and chargebacks
  • Providing documented, auditable completion

Direct or reversible payment methods, including peer-to-peer transfers or post-transfer payment arrangements, are not accepted.
